Donor privacy policy

The National Center for Missing & Exploited Children® will not sell, trade or share a donor’s personal information with anyone else or send donor mailings on behalf of other organizations.

At any point donors may contact NCMEC’s Development Department to remove their names and contact information from mailings.

For more information contact:

National Center for Missing & Exploited Children Attn: Director of Development Charles B. Wang International Children’s Building 699 Prince Street Alexandria, VA 22314-3175 Phone: 1-866-411-KIDS (5437)

Accurate personal information

If you are a donor and would like to update, review or delete your personal information on file with NCMEC please use the form below. Be sure to include both your old and new personal information in the request so records can be properly updated.
