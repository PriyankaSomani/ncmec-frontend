Matching Gifts

Let your company double or triple your impact! Many companies have a matching gift program which matches the same amount or more of donations you make to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children®. All you have to do is ask.

Here’s how you can double or triple your impact with workplace donations:

1. Donate

Make a cash, check or credit card donation to NCMEC. Keep your gift receipt in case your company requires it. Most corporate matching programs give you up to one year to request their match on your donation.

2. Search

Search in the form on the right for the name of your company. Select your regional office if you get more than one result. If you do not see your company listed, check with your HR department to ask if your company has a matching gift program.

3. Match

There are two ways to submit the matching request. You can download your company’s matching gift verification form using the link provided from your search, print it, fill it out and mail to:

NCMEC
Attn: Development
Charles B. Wang International Children’s Building
699 Prince Street
Alexandria, VA 22314-3175

Alternately, if your company provides a link to their intranet, log in and submit your matching gift request. Your company will process and send it to NCMEC.

Once received NCMEC takes care of the rest. Your company will send its matching donation directly to NCMEC and you will double or even triple your impact.

Questions? Email: development@ncmec.org
